Deshaun Walker

Artist

I am currently an artist/designer located in Atlanta, Georgia. Experimentation has always been something that defines why I make work. There is a form of freedom that comes with experimentation that I am constantly chasing, the freedom to create. My unique style is a product of his upbringing, allowing him to bring his work to life. From the influence of cartoons, religion, art, and teachings he was brought up on, I uses his personal experience to create his reflection on modernity.
